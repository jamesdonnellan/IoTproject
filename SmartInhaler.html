How does SmartInhaler work?

We multiple sensors to constantly measure enviornmental readings to help you understand and manage your asthma. My IoT device aim to battle asthma by bringing down the risk of asthma influenced death.

Reading Recieved

The device will show current live readings of both dust and pollen levels in your current location, this will notify users of when it is reccomended to take a puff from their inhaler.

Breathe Easier

After reading the live readings from our device, take your inhaler to prevent any discomfort in the chest/ breathing.
