About Us

We use innovative technology to help you understand and manage your asthma. Our devices aim to battle asthma by bringing down the risk of asthma influenced death.

Our Services

Enviornmental hazard detection

Our sensors detect dangerous pollen and dust levels that could potentially bring on sudden atshma attacks.By tracking PM 2.5 and 1.0 levels we alert you before issues arise.

Alert Notifications

Our website alerts you of current pollen and dust levels that may affect your atshma. Our innovative software sends instant warnings on our website for when you should use your inhaler based off of current enviornmental readings.

Data Storing

We store your data in order to understand your asthma better, we keep track of when you use your inhaler most so we can send you a personalised notification when you need it the most.
